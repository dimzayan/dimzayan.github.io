Dim WaaS

We are all Satoshi.

House of WaaS is an art project series created by Dim that explores and celebrates the emotional relationship between Bitcoin and individuals. It honours the original Bitcoin whitepaper's creator, Satoshi Nakamoto, who published it in 2008. Nakamoto disappeared, but his legacy is here to stay.

The Bitcoin Voicepaper

First part of WaaS is The Voicepaper, a collaborative video recording of the Bitcoin whitepaper narrated by Bitcoiners. Each contributor recites a section from Satoshi’s document and pay their respect to what and who started it all.

This project is first and foremost a celebration of the Bitcoin community, of its strength and unity, as well as its broad diversity in backgrounds, origins and aspirations. It is a statement that Bitcoin belongs to everyone, and that the meaning of the “Vires in Numeris” motto is contextually as computational as it is human.
