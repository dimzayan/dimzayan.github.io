A Bitcoin Fable by Dim Zayan

BIT'S JOURNEY

Bulltardia is a fantasy fable following the journey of Bit the Honey Badger on his way to the moon.

Even though the story is set in an imaginary world, most of the animals and places you’re about to discover are based on real people and events that relate to the world of Bitcoin and cryptocurrency.

But you don’t have to be know  anything about Bitcoin  to enjoy Bulltardia.

SATOSHI'S TREASURE.

The twelve words of the mnemonic passphrase of a BTC address holding 10 million satoshis are hiding in the story throughout Season One. Will you be the first to find the hidden keys?

  • The first key is one of the reasons the Honey Badger don't care. Developed over time, It is a six letter adjective that starts with a "i".
  • If Bit was to listen to Shepard Fairey, he would do the second key.
  • The third key defines the badger's journey.
  • The fourth key is an interaction between Bit and bull in episode 3 and ddone by a bird in episode 4. It starts with a "W"
