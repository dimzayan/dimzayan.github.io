About Sensational Geographic

Each work is created over a page from vintage National Geographic magazines from the 40s and 50s. I hijack forgotten stories of either ancient civilizations or post-war industrialization and repurpose them into our contemporary Bitcoin history by showcasing the spectrum of emotions and characters seen in the community, mostly depicted as bulls (as is the one you picked)
